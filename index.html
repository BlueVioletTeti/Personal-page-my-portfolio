Weather app

Users can search for the weather data in the location of interest or at their current location. Temperature could be displaced in Celsius or Fahrenheit depending on the user preferences. The wind speed will be displaced in meter per second units if the temperature is displayed in Celsius, and in miles per second if the temperature is displayed in Fahrenheit. Additionally, this app provides weather forecast for the following 6 days.
It is final project for SheCodes Plus course
